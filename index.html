第一部分：絕對核心定理 (Theorem Statements)

這些定理必須能用英文寫出完整的數學敘述（假設 Let... + 結論 Then...）。

1. Central Limit Theorem (CLT) 必背 Let \(X_1, ..., X_n \overset{iid}{\sim} (\mu, \sigma^2)\). Then as \(n \to \infty\): $$ \frac{\bar{X}-\mu}{\sigma/\sqrt{n}} \xrightarrow{d} N(0,1) $$
2. Factorization Theorem (分解定理) 必背 A statistic \(T(X)\) is sufficient for \(\theta\) if and only if the joint pdf/pmf can be factored as: $$ f(x|\theta) = g(T(x)|\theta)h(x) $$
用途：尋找充分統計量最快的方法。
3. Neyman-Pearson Lemma (N-P 引理) 必背 For testing \(H_0: \theta=\theta_0\) vs \(H_1: \theta=\theta_1\), the test with rejection region \(R\) given by: $$ x \in R \iff \frac{L(\theta_0)}{L(\theta_1)} \le k $$ is the Most Powerful (MP) test of size \(\alpha\), where \(k\) is determined by \(P(x \in R | H_0) = \alpha\).
4. Rao-Blackwell Theorem 必背 Let \(\hat{\theta}\) be an unbiased estimator and \(T\) be a sufficient statistic for \(\theta\). Define \(\phi(T) = E[\hat{\theta}|T]\). Then: 1. \(\phi(T)\) is unbiased: \(E[\phi(T)] = \theta\). 2. \(Var(\phi(T)) \le Var(\hat{\theta})\).
用途：改進估計量，使其變異數變小。
5. Lehmann-Scheffé Theorem 必背 If \(T\) is a complete and sufficient statistic for \(\theta\), and \(\phi(T)\) is an unbiased estimator of \(\theta\), then \(\phi(T)\) is the unique UMVUE of \(\theta\).
用途：尋找最佳不偏估計量 (Best Unbiased Estimator)。
6. Gauss-Markov Theorem 必背 In a linear regression model \(Y = X\beta + \epsilon\) with \(E[\epsilon]=0\), \(Var(\epsilon)=\sigma^2 I\) (homoscedasticity, no serial correlation), the Ordinary Least Squares (OLS) estimators are BLUE (Best Linear Unbiased Estimators).
7. Cochran’s Theorem Let \(Z_1, ..., Z_n \overset{iid}{\sim} N(0,1)\). If \(\sum Z_i^2 = Q_1 + ... + Q_k\), then \(Q_i \sim \chi^2(v_i)\) are independent if and only if \(\sum v_i = n\).
用途：推導 \(\bar{X}\) 與 \(S^2\) 獨立，以及 \((n-1)S^2/\sigma^2 \sim \chi^2(n-1)\)。

第二部分：重要名詞定義 (Definitions)

適用於 "Explain the term" 或 "True/False" 題型。包含核心與潛力考點。

A. 統計推論基礎 (Inference)

p-value (p值) 必背
The probability of observing a test statistic as extreme as, or more extreme than, the one observed, assuming \(H_0\) is true.
Type I Error (\(\alpha\)) / Type II Error (\(\beta\))
Type I: Rejecting \(H_0\) when \(H_0\) is true.
Type II: Failing to reject \(H_0\) when \(H_1\) is true.
Power of a Test (\(1-\beta\))
The probability of rejecting \(H_0\) when \(H_1\) is true. (即檢定力)
Consistent Estimator (一致估計量) 必背
An estimator \(\hat{\theta}_n\) such that \(\hat{\theta}_n \xrightarrow{P} \theta\) as \(n \to \infty\). (即樣本數無限大時，估計量機率收斂至真值)。
Sufficient Statistic (充分統計量)
A statistic \(T(X)\) that contains all the information in the sample about \(\theta\). Formally, the conditional distribution of the sample given \(T\) is independent of \(\theta\).
Complete Statistic (完備統計量) 潛力
A statistic \(T\) is complete if \(E[g(T)] = 0\) for all \(\theta\) implies \(P(g(T)=0) = 1\) for all \(\theta\). (UMVUE 的先決條件)。
Likelihood Ratio Statistic (\(\lambda\)) 潛力
$$ \lambda(x) = \frac{\sup_{\theta \in \Theta_0} L(\theta|x)}{\sup_{\theta \in \Theta} L(\theta|x)} $$ (檢定統計量的理論源頭，如 F test)。
Mean Squared Error (MSE) 潛力
\(MSE(\hat{\theta}) = E[(\hat{\theta}-\theta)^2] = Var(\hat{\theta}) + [Bias(\hat{\theta})]^2\). (必背此分解式)。
Fisher Information 潛力
\(I(\theta) = -E\left[ \frac{\partial^2}{\partial \theta^2} \ln f(X;\theta) \right]\). (計算 CRLB 用)。

B. 迴歸分析 (Regression)

Multicollinearity (共線性) 必背
High correlation among independent variables (\(X\)'s), leading to unstable \(\hat{\beta}\) estimates and large standard errors.
Heteroscedasticity (異質變異數)
The variance of error terms is not constant (\(Var(\epsilon_i) \neq \sigma^2\)).
Autocorrelation (自我相關) 必背
Error terms are correlated over time (e.g., \(Cov(\epsilon_t, \epsilon_{t-1}) \neq 0\)). Commonly found in time series data.
Coefficient of Determination (\(R^2\))
The proportion of the total variation in \(Y\) that is explained by the regression model (\(SSR/SSTO\)).
Residual vs. Error 潛力
Error (\(\epsilon\)): \(Y - E[Y]\) (Unobservable, true population error).
Residual (\(e\)): \(Y - \hat{Y}\) (Observable, sample estimation error).

C. 機率與隨機變數 (Probability)

Conditional Expectation
\(E[Y|X]\) is a random variable that represents the average value of \(Y\) for a given \(X\).
Moment Generating Function (MGF) 潛力
\(M_X(t) = E[e^{tX}]\). Uniquely determines the distribution.
Convergence in Probability vs. Distribution 潛力
\(\xrightarrow{P}\): Limit of probability (\(P(|X_n-X|>\epsilon) \to 0\)).
\(\xrightarrow{d}\): Limit of CDF (\(F_n(x) \to F(x)\)).

第三部分：必備數理推導技巧 (SOP)

1. 尋找 UMVUE (三步殺)

  1. Factorization Thm: 找出充分統計量 \(T\)。
  2. Unbiasedness: 找一個不偏估計量（或是修正 \(T\) 使其不偏）。
  3. Lehmann-Scheffé: 宣稱 \(T\) 是完備的（指數族性質），則該不偏估計量即為 UMVUE。

2. AR(1) 模型變異數推導 (必背結論)

若 \( \epsilon_t = \rho \epsilon_{t-1} + u_t \)，其中 \(u_t \sim N(0, \sigma_u^2)\) 且 \(|\rho|<1\)，則：

$$ Var(\epsilon_t) = \frac{\sigma_u^2}{1-\rho^2} $$ $$ Cov(\epsilon_t, \epsilon_{t-k}) = \frac{\sigma_u^2}{1-\rho^2} \rho^k $$

3. Cramér-Rao Lower Bound (CRLB)

$$ Var(\hat{\theta}) \ge \frac{1}{n I(\theta)}, \quad I(\theta) = -E\left[ \frac{\partial^2 \ln f(X;\theta)}{\partial \theta^2} \right] $$
